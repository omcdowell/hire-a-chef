And it's completely doable

Where we find them

CCA Manila (the culinary school) places trained private chefs with households, and reputable agencies do too -- vetted, with a replacement if it's not the right fit.

Above board

A proper contract under the Philippine Kasambahay Law -- fair pay, a rest day, real benefits. We do this right, not cash-in-hand.

One simple pot

We pool a monthly kitty: chef + groceries, divided four ways. One transfer each, and dinner is handled for a whole month.

What we're actually buying

It isn't really about the money. It's about what every evening turns into.
